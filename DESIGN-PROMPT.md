# Design Prompt — Dr Somani's Homoeopathy Website

> Reusable brief used to generate / regenerate this website.

## Master Prompt

Build a modern, trust-inspiring, **mobile-first** website for **"Dr Somani's Homoeopathy"**,
a homoeopathy clinic run by **Dr Kushal Somani** with **27 years of experience**.

**Look & feel:** calm, holistic-healing aesthetic.
- Palette: deep healing green `#2E7D5B`, warm cream `#FAF7F0`, soft gold accent `#C9A24B`.
- Fonts: **Poppins** for headings, **Inter** for body.
- Rounded soft cards, gentle shadows, generous whitespace, subtle leaf/natural motifs.

**Structure (top to bottom):**
1. **Slim dark-green top utility bar** — phone (+91 98341 72124), email (kushal.a.somani@gmail.com),
   locations (Pune & Jalgaon), Instagram (@somanikushal), and an "Online Consultations Available" badge.
2. **Sticky translucent navbar** — logo "Dr Somani's Homoeopathy", links (Home, About, Treatments,
   Why Homoeopathy, Testimonials, Contact) and a gold **"Book Appointment"** CTA. Hamburger menu on mobile.
3. **Hero** — headline *"27 Years of Trusted Homoeopathic Care"*, supporting line about safe,
   side-effect-free, root-cause treatment, two CTAs (**Book Appointment** + **WhatsApp Now**),
   and a doctor profile card.
4. **Trust-stats strip** — 27+ Years · 8 Treatments · 2 Clinics · 100% side-effect-free.
5. **About** — Dr Somani's experience, clinics, online care.
6. **Treatments grid (8 cards w/ icons):** Skin Diseases & Vitiligo, Allergies, Migraine, PCOD,
   Kidney Stones, Acidity & Digestion, Paediatric Illnesses, Mental Health Care.
7. **Why Homoeopathy** — safe & side-effect-free, boosts immunity, long-lasting results, personalised care.
8. **Testimonials.**
9. **Contact** — both clinic locations (Pune & Jalgaon), online-consultation note, and an enquiry
   form that opens a pre-filled WhatsApp message to the clinic.
10. **Footer** + floating sticky **WhatsApp + Call** buttons.

**Requirements:** fully responsive, accessible, fast static HTML/CSS/JS, no build step.
